About Me

Felicia N. Huang is a research fellow at the Lee Kuan Yew Centre for Innovative Cities (LKYCIC). Prior to joining LKYCIC she received her Ph.D. from the Singapore Management Unviersity’s School of Information Systems. Her research interests center on urban informatics, specifically its application on urban health and place identity. View Felicia's CV .
