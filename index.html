About Me

Felicia N. Huang is a research data manager/engineer at the MRC Unit for Lifelong Health and Ageing at the University College London (UCL). Prior to joining UCL, she worked at the Lee Kuan Yew Centre for Innovative Cities (LKYCIC) as a research fellow. She received her Ph.D. from the Singapore Management University’s School of Information Systems. View Felicia's CV .
